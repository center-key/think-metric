It's entertaining to poke fun at the clumsiness of imperial units, but the economic costs are real.  The numeracy of school kids in America is harmed by the disjoint nature of imperial units, and the ability of school kids to intuitively and quickly work with measurements is diminished. 

The Oddball

It's no accident that 1 milliliter is 1 cubic centimeter and conveniently has a mass of 1 gram when filled with water.
