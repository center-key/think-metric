One of the most effective ways to become familiar with metric units is to configure your mobile device and laptop to display measurements using the metric system instead of imperial.  After you've make the switch, try to avoid doing unit conversions.  You'll find that you gain familiarity with metric surprisingly quickly by just using it. 

Instructions

  • Apple iPhone and iPad (iOS)
  • Android (AOSP)
  • Apple MacBook (macOS)
